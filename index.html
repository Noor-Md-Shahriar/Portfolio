About Me

I am Noor Md Shahriar, Senior Lecturer in the Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering at the University of Global Village (UGV), Barishal, Bangladesh. I completed my B.Sc. in Engineering from Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET) and am currently pursuing an M.Sc. in Engineering at RUET.

With over 4 years of teaching experience, my research focuses on metamaterial absorbers. I aim to pursue a Ph.D. abroad and further my contributions to academia and research.
