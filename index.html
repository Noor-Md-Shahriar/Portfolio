About Me

Hello! I am Noor Md Shahriar, a Senior Lecturer in Electrical & Electronics Engineering at the University of Global Village (UGV), Barishal, Bangladesh. I specialize in metamaterial absorbers, machine learning, and electromagnetic applications. I hold a B.Sc. degree from Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET) and am currently pursuing an M.Sc. in Engineering.
