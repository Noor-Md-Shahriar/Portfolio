About Me

I am Noor Md Shahriar, a Senior Lecturer in the Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering at the University of Global Village (UGV), Barishal, Bangladesh. I completed my B.Sc. in Engineering from Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET) and am currently pursuing an M.Sc. in Engineering at RUET. My research focuses on metamaterial absorbers, and I aim to pursue a Ph.D. abroad to further contribute to academia and research.
